Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first team? +

To create your first team, select an upcoming match and click the "Create Team" button. Choose 11 players within the credit limit, ensuring you follow the team composition rules. Don't forget to select a captain and vice-captain!

What happens if a match is cancelled? +

If a match is cancelled or abandoned, all contests related to that match will be cancelled, and entry fees will be refunded to your account balance within 24 hours.

How are points calculated? +

Points are calculated based on players' actual performance in the match. This includes runs scored, wickets taken, catches, run-outs, and other cricket statistics. Captain's points are multiplied by 2x and Vice-captain's by 1.5x.

When can I withdraw my winnings? +

Winnings can be withdrawn once the match and contest results are officially declared. The minimum withdrawal amount is ₹200, and the process typically takes 24-48 hours to complete.

Can I create multiple teams? +

Yes, you can create multiple teams for the same match, but you can only join a contest once with one team. Different contests may have different rules regarding multiple team entries.
